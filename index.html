M. Visvesvaraya

Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, often simply referred to as Sir MV, was a renowned Indian engineer, statesman, and visionary. He played a pivotal role in modernizing and industrializing India during the early 20th century. Sir MV's exceptional contributions to engineering and nation-building have left a lasting legacy, and he is widely celebrated for his role in infrastructure development and as a prominent public servant
